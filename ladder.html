Ladder Rules

  1. Players can challenge others ranked up to 2 positions above them.
  2. If the challenger wins, they swap positions with the player they challenged.
  3. Players must accept challenges within 7 days.
  4. Matches should be best of 5 games.
  5. Results must be recorded promptly after the match.
